# ♻️ Spaced Repetition – Review Modules 01–04

## 🧪 Task
1. Create two tables: `authors (id, name)` and `books (id, title, author_id, price)`.
2. Insert at least 3 authors and 5 books, one author with no books.
3. Write an `INNER JOIN` to list books with author names.
4. Write a `LEFT JOIN` that lists all authors and their books (include authors with NULL books).
5. Write a query that shows books with a price higher than the average price of all books.
6. Use a `UNION` to combine a list of author names and book titles (just for practice).
