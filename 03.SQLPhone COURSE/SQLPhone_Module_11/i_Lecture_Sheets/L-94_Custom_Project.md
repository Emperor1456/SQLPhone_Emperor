# 📖 L‑94 – Custom Project

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE
Design and implement a database of your choice,
applying all Module 11 patterns.

## 🧱 BRICK 1 – Choosing a Domain
Pick a domain that interests you:
- Fitness tracker (workouts, exercises)
- Music playlist manager (songs, playlists)
- Task manager (projects, tasks, users)
- Any other idea.

## 🧱 BRICK 2 – Requirements
1. At least 4 related tables.
2. At least one many‑to‑many relationship.
3. At least one CHECK or UNIQUE constraint.
4. Seed data with at least 5 rows per table.
5. Write 5 meaningful queries demonstrating joins,
   aggregation, and filtering.

## 💡 Why This Matters
This is your portfolio piece – a complete,
self‑directed project that showcases all your skills.

## 📌 Key Takeaway
The best way to prove mastery is to create
something from scratch. This project is yours.

*Build what excites you; the database will follow.*
